A Discussion of the Data Characteristics

In a relational paradigm, the primary data characteristics are the selectivity of attributes (important for simple selection operations) and the join selectivity (important for join operations). In an XML paradigm, there are several complicating characteristics to consider as discussed in Section Depth and Fanout and Data Set Granularity.
